9th Annual Turkey Giveaway – 2025

Our 2025 goal is to distribute 1,000 turkeys, aiming to be Western New York's largest giveaway.

Since 2016, Bridge Niagara Foundation has hosted annual turkey giveaways, growing from a grassroots effort into a beloved community tradition.

In 2024, volunteers distributed over 500 turkeys to local families, reflecting the event's expanding reach.

“Our sponsors make this possible,” said Anas Mangla of Mangla Financial Services.
“Giving back is our commitment,” shared Naz Akhtar of Akhtar Realty.
