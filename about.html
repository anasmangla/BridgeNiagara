About Bridge Niagara Foundation

Bridge Niagara Foundation connects resources with compassion to uplift Niagara County through community programs and support. Our board and volunteers are committed to building a more just and connected community.

Our Mission

We strive to bridge gaps in services for families by providing food assistance, educational opportunities, and neighborhood support driven by kindness and collaboration.

Our Story

Founded by neighbors in Niagara Falls, Bridge Niagara began as a small volunteer effort and has grown into a community foundation offering year-round programs like the annual Turkey Giveaway, vendor shows, art workshops, and more.
