About Us

Our Mission

Bridge Niagara Foundation improves lives in Niagara County through education, wellness, and opportunity. We serve the underrepresented and under-resourced communities of Western New York through outreach programs, community support, and youth engagement. Join us in bridging the gap with dignity, service, and love.
