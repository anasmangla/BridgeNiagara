Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Bridge Niagara Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. When you donate, you will receive a receipt via email that can be used when filing your taxes. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for full guidance.

Can I donate monthly?

Absolutely. When filling out our donation form, you have the option to set up a recurring monthly gift. Monthly donations allow us to plan more effectively and provide consistent support to the families we serve.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We use Stripe, one of the most secure payment platforms available. Your credit card and personal information are encrypted and never stored on our website. We also use HTTPS to ensure secure data transfer.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, an email receipt will be sent immediately after your donation is completed. This receipt will include the donation amount, transaction ID, and our tax-exempt ID. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, please check your spam folder or contact us.

Do you accept check or cash donations?

Yes, we accept both. You can mail checks to our office address listed on the Contact page. For cash donations, please contact us to arrange a secure handoff. A receipt can be issued in person or by email.

Can I sponsor a full program?

Yes, individuals and organizations can sponsor specific events or full programs such as our Turkey Giveaway or Youth Camps. Sponsorship opportunities come with promotional recognition. Contact us to learn about current sponsorship tiers.

Can I volunteer?

Definitely. Volunteers are vital to the success of our programs. Whether you want to help with event logistics, youth engagement, or community outreach, we have a spot for you. Visit the Volunteer page to submit your interest.
