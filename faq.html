Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up to volunteer?

Visit our volunteer page to see current opportunities and register.

Where are you located and how can I contact you?

Our office is at 6951 Williams Road, Niagara Falls, NY 14304. Email info@bridgeniagara.org or use our contact form.

Are donations tax-deductible and will I receive a receipt?

Yes. Bridge Niagara Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You will receive an email receipt after donating; contact us if you need a copy.

How are donations used?

Donations support community programs such as the Turkey Giveaway, vendor shows, art sessions, and neighborhood cleanups.

What is your mission and vision?

We connect resources with compassion to empower families and build a stronger Niagara community.

What programs do you offer?

Our programs include the annual Turkey Giveaway, vendor shows, community art sessions, and area cleanups. Visit the programs page for details.

How can businesses sponsor events or participate as vendors?

Local businesses can sponsor events or join our vendor shows. Reach out at info@bridgeniagara.org to learn more.

When is the Turkey Giveaway?

Registration opens each October and distribution happens the week before Thanksgiving.

How can I stay informed about upcoming events?

Check our website regularly and follow Bridge Niagara Foundation on social media for the latest news.
