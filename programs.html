Bollywood Night & Farzana Legacy Scholarship

150 attendees joined for prayers led by Anas Mangla and remarks from Nasreen Akhtar before a lively Bhangra Buffalo performance. Scholarships were awarded to Arooj Kynat and Saba Ali.

Farzana Legacy Scholarship

Honoring the life of Farzana Begum, this scholarship uplifts students devoted to community service. 2023 recipients were Aisha Makama, Sally Rick, and Sara Kim-Kubiak.
