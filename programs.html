Bollywood Night & Farzana Legacy Scholarship Dinner – May 2024

Over 150 attendees enjoyed vibrant cultural performances while scholarships honored the legacy of Farzana Begum.

Turkey Giveaway

In 2024, volunteers distributed more than 500 turkeys. Our 2025 goal is 1,000 turkeys for local families.

Vendor Shows

Seasonal fairs and bazaars empower minority and women-owned businesses to showcase their goods and services.

Community Art Sessions – July 2024

Families gathered at the Niagara Arts & Cultural Center to create murals, leaving twenty canvases to brighten public corridors.

Area Cleanups & Service Projects – April 2024

At Gill Creek Park, volunteers filled forty bags of debris, making pathways safer for nearby families.

Business Meetups & Workshops

Naz716 hosts networking sessions while Aero Transportation supports 2024 reopening workshops for aspiring entrepreneurs.
